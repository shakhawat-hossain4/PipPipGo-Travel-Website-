Bongobondhu Safari Park Tour

Discover the Wonders of Bongobondhu Safari Park

Embark on an exciting one-day trip from Dhaka to Bongobondhu Safari Park in Gazipur. This wildlife sanctuary offers a thrilling experience with animals roaming freely in vast enclosures. Ideal for families and nature lovers, this tour promises a day filled with adventure, learning, and fun.

Package Highlights:

  • Guided safari tours through the wildlife sanctuary
  • Spot a variety of animals, including tigers, lions, and zebras
  • Visit the aviary to see exotic birds
  • Explore the Butterfly Garden and other attractions
  • Picnic lunch amidst the serene environment

Package Details:

  • Duration: 1 Day (7:00 AM - 6:00 PM)
  • Price: 2500 BDT per person
  • Includes: Transportation from Dhaka, entry tickets, guided safari, lunch, and refreshments

Itinerary:

  • 7:00 AM: Departure from Dhaka
  • 9:00 AM: Arrival at Bongobondhu Safari Park
  • 10:00 AM: Start of guided safari tour
  • 1:00 PM: Picnic lunch in the park
  • 2:30 PM: Visit the Aviary, Butterfly Garden, and other attractions
  • 4:30 PM: Free time to explore or relax
  • 5:00 PM: Departure from Safari Park
  • 6:00 PM: Return to Dhaka
