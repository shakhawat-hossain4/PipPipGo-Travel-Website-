Sundarbans Forest Tour

Explore the Sundarbans Forest

The Sundarbans is a magical mangrove forest, home to the Bengal tiger and a rich diversity of wildlife. Our 5-day tour package offers an immersive experience into this natural wonder, with boat safaris, local cultural visits, and more.

Package Highlights:

  • Boat safari through the heart of the Sundarbans
  • Wildlife spotting, including the Bengal tiger
  • Visits to local villages and cultural immersion
  • Guided tours by local experts
  • Birdwatching in mangrove forests

Package Details:

  • Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights
  • Price: 8000 BDT per person
  • Accommodation: 3-star hotel
  • Includes: Accommodation, meals, transportation, boat safari, guide services

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival and transfer to the Sundarbans
  • Day 2: Boat safari and wildlife spotting
  • Day 3: Village visits and cultural experiences
  • Day 4: Mangrove forest exploration and birdwatching
  • Day 5: Departure
