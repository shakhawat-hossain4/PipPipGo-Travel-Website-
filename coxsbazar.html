Cox's Bazar Beach Tour

Experience the Beauty of Cox's Bazar

Join us on an exciting 4-day tour of the longest sea beach in the world, Cox's Bazar. Enjoy the serene beauty, water activities, local culture, and much more during your stay.

Package Highlights:

  • Beach activities including surfing, swimming, and sunbathing
  • Visit to Himchari and Inani Beach
  • Explore the local markets and seafood restaurants
  • Guided tour of Maheshkhali Island
  • Sunset views from Laboni Beach

Package Details:

  • Duration: 4 Days / 3 Nights
  • Price: 6000 BDT per person
  • Accommodation: 4-star beachfront hotel
  • Includes: Accommodation, meals, transportation, guided tours

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival in Cox's Bazar and beach activities
  • Day 2: Excursion to Himchari and Inani Beach
  • Day 3: Boat trip to Maheshkhali Island and local market exploration
  • Day 4: Departure after morning at Laboni Beach
